WorkMate Pet

Your desktop companion that grows with your work

About

WorkMate Pet is a desktop time tracking app that makes productivity fun. Unlike boring time trackers, it combines automatic work-time tracking with a cute virtual pet that evolves as you focus on your work.

Features

  • Automatically tracks your work time in selected programs
  • Only counts time when you're actually typing or clicking
  • Watch your pet level up and evolve as you work
  • Fire effects and encouragement messages when you focus
  • Let your pet walk around your screen freely
  • Settings and progress saved automatically

How It Works

  1. Select which programs you use for work (e.g., VSCode, Photoshop, Chrome)
  2. The app automatically detects when you're working in those programs
  3. Your work time becomes experience points that level up your pet
  4. Get encouragement messages and visual effects as you focus

Tech Stack

Electron, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Node.js, active-win, uiohook-napi, electron-store
